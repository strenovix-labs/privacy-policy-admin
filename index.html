Privacy & Data Stewardship

Effective: March 24, 2026
Real-time geofencing

1. Information we collect

Personal & electoral

  • Name, Phone, EPIC number – verified voter identity
  • Gender, Age/DOB – demographic insights
  • Disability type & assistance flags

Sensitive location

  • Latitude/Longitude (officer duty mode)
  • Block, Taluk, District, Pincode

Operational data

  • Voting intention & crew allocation
  • DAP mapping & booth readiness

2. How we utilize data

  • Dynamic voter mapping & heat layers
  • Highlight DAP citizens for priority assistance
  • Officer dashboards & query resolution
  • Election command center (RO/ARO)
  • Firebase notifications for critical alerts
  • Operational efficiency & resource planning

3. Geofencing & live location

Authorized officers share real-time location only during active election shifts to:

  • 📍 Display nearby voters requiring mobility aid
  • 🗺️ Optimize field routes for BLOs & response teams
  • 🔒 Data encrypted, restricted to supervisory roles

Location can be disabled anytime — some features may be limited.

4. Trusted third‑parties

Google Maps Platform Firebase Cloud Messaging

Strict data minimization agreements — no voter information is sold or shared for marketing. All integrations comply with electoral data protection standards.

5. Infrastructure & encryption

  • Database: PostgreSQL + RBAC (role-based)
  • Hosting: Railway (SOC2 Type II)
  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 / AES-256 at rest
  • JWT + MFA for admin roles, continuous audits

6. No data commercialization

VoteAble never sells, rents, or trades any personal or electoral data. Access is restricted to verified election officers (BLO, RO, ARO, Super Admin). Government data sharing only occurs under legal mandates with strict confidentiality.

7. Children's privacy

Strictly for users 18+ (voters and officers). No data is knowingly collected from individuals under 13. Any incidental data is purged immediately.

8. Your data rights

  • Access: Secure dashboards for officers
  • Rectification: Update voter details via BLO portal
  • Erasure: Request removal (subject to election law)
  • Contact Data Protection Officer for any request

9. Policy evolution

This policy may be updated to reflect legal, technological or operational improvements. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice and email to registered officers.
