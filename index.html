Section 01
About VoteAble

VoteAble is a secure, government-grade election management and voter assistance platform exclusively deployed for authorized election personnel. The system is not a consumer product and is restricted to verified officers of the election administration.

Authorized User Roles

  • Booth Level Officers (BLO)
  • Returning Officers (RO)
  • Assistant Returning Officers (ARO)
  • District Super Administrators
VoteAble facilitates field-level coordination, voter assistance tracking, and geospatial visualization of voter needs — particularly for Differently Abled Persons (DAP).

System Owner: Election Administration (Internal Use)
Developer Contact: strenovix@gmail.com

Section 03
Purpose of Processing

All data processed within VoteAble is used exclusively for the lawful administration of elections and provision of voter assistance services. There is no secondary commercial use of any kind.

  • Identifying voters who require disability-related assistance
  • Displaying Differently Abled Persons (DAP) on secure officer map interfaces
  • Enabling election officers to locate and assist voters efficiently
  • Managing voter queries and tracking resolution progress
  • Supporting decision-making via administrative dashboards
  • Coordinating field-level activities on election day
No Commercial Use No Advertising No Profiling No Data Sale
Section 04
Location Transparency
Important Disclosure: This app collects precise location data from election officers during active foreground use only.

Officer Location Usage

  • Collected only while app is open and in active use (foreground)
  • Used solely to show proximity to voters requiring assistance
  • Enables real-time operational coordination between field officers
  • Never collected in the background
  • Not permanently stored — session-use only

Voter Location

Voter location data is stored as part of official election records and is used exclusively for mapping assistance requirements and dispatch planning. Access is restricted to authorized officers by role-based controls.

Location data is never shared with advertisers, or any external commercial entities.

Section 08
Storage & Retention

Storage Infrastructure

All data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database hosted via Railway.app infrastructure. All connections are encrypted using TLS. No sensitive data is stored in plaintext or locally on user devices.

Retention Policy

  • Voter assistance data: retained for the duration of the election cycle
  • Officer session/location data: not permanently stored — purged after session
  • Operational logs: retained as required by election administration guidelines
  • Data purge requests processed through official administrative channels
Retention timelines are aligned with official election operational requirements and applicable data protection regulations.
Section 09
User Rights & DPDP Act

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), data principals are entitled to the following rights, exercisable through official administrative channels:

  • Right to Access: View personal data held about you in the system
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of data where legally permissible
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Raise concerns with the designated Data Fiduciary
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate a representative in case of incapacity

Data modification is governed by assigned administrative role privileges. All requests are processed through formal election administrative channels.

Section 10
Security Measures

VoteAble implements industry-standard security controls appropriate for a government election management system.

Technical Controls

  • HTTPS / TLS encryption for all client-server communication
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) — officers access only data relevant to their role
  • Secured and isolated database environment
  • No sensitive data cached or stored locally on Android devices
  • Authentication required before any data access

Operational Controls

  • Restricted administrative access — principle of least privilege
  • Audit trails for data access and modifications
  • Regular security reviews aligned with election schedules
