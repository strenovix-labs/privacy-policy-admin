Officer-grade data protection

VoteAble · Privacy & Data Ethics

  Effective date: 24th March 2026  |    For BLO, RO, ARO, Super Admin

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Name (Voter, Booth Level Officer, Returning Officer, ARO)
  • Phone Number – secured and role-based access
  • Voter ID (EPIC Number) – unique electoral identifier
  • Gender & Age/Date of Birth – for demographic accuracy

b) Sensitive & Location Information

  • Geographic coordinates (Latitude & Longitude) – real-time field coordination
  • Address details: Block, Taluk, District, Pincode – precinct mapping

c) Additional Assistance Data

  • Disability type (if any) – to ensure accessible voting
  • Voting intention (Will Vote / Won’t Vote) – analytical outreach
  • Assistance requirements & crew/officer details for election duty

2. How We Use the Information

  •   Provide real-time voter mapping and geospatial visualization
  •   Display Differently Abled Persons (DAP) on interactive maps for assistance
  •   Enable officers to assess voter needs and optimize booth support
  •   Allow BLOs to manage/resolve voter queries through dashboard
  •   Deliver election management dashboards for RO/ARO & Super Admin
  •   Send targeted push notifications (Firebase) for critical updates
  •   Improve operational efficiency and resource allocation

3. Location & Geofencing

VoteAble collects real-time location of authorized admin users (BLO, RO, crew) exclusively during active election duties. This enables:

  • 📍 Showing nearby voters requiring mobility or DAP assistance
  • 🗺️ Optimizing field routes and immediate response
  • 🔒 Location data is encrypted, never shared with external entities, and visible only to supervisory roles.

Officers may disable location services at any time, but certain features may be limited.

4. Trusted Third-Party Services

Google Maps API – secure rendering of electoral maps, location intelligence
Firebase Cloud Messaging – instant push notifications for election alerts

All third-party integrations adhere to strict data minimization and confidentiality agreements. No voter/officer data is sold or transferred for marketing.

5. Secure Infrastructure

  • Database: PostgreSQL with role-based access controls (RBAC)
  • Hosting: Railway (SOC2-compliant environment)
  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest for sensitive fields
  • Continuous vulnerability monitoring and strict authentication (JWT + MFA for admin roles)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing

6. Data Sharing & Confidentiality

We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal or electoral data. Information is accessible only to designated election officers (BLO, RO, ARO, Super Admin) for official purposes. Data sharing with government election bodies is compliant with local electoral laws and only upon lawful request.

7. Children's Privacy

VoteAble is strictly intended for election management and voters above 18 years of age. The application does not knowingly collect information from individuals under 13. If such data is discovered, it is immediately removed from active systems.

8. Your Data Rights

  • Right to Access: Authorized officers can view data via secure dashboards.
  • Right to Rectification: Update voter profiles, contact details through BLO portal.
  • Right to Erasure: Subject to electoral retention laws, you may request data removal.
  • For any data request, reach out to our Data Protection Officer at the contact below.

9. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be revised to reflect technological changes, legal updates, or new features. The “Effective Date” at the top will be revised accordingly. Users will be notified through in-app announcements or email for material changes.
